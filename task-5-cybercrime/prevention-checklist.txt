       
**STAY  SAFE  ONLINE** 

☐ Use strong, unique passwords for email, social media, and banking apps
☐ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts
☐ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking, UPI, or logging into sensitive accounts
☐ Do not click on unknown links or download files from unfamiliar sources
☐ Check privacy settings on social media and limit personal information sharing
☐ Verify internship/job offers before sharing personal or academic details
☐ Avoid sharing OTP, passwords, or PIN with anyone, even if they claim to be from bank
☐ Never approve unknown UPI collect requests (Request Money notifications)
☐ Do not scan random QR codes sent by strangers for receiving money
☐ Double-check UPI ID or phone number before sending money

*Report immediately at National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
*Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (for financial fraud & cybercrime)
