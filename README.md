# Project Overview
This project focuses on developing digital literacy skills through a series of five practical tasks. The project covers important areas such as digital tools, online platforms, professional communication, portfolio creation, and cyber safety. Each task is designed to enhance understanding of responsible and effective use of digital technologies. The repository contains presentation materials, platform activities, email etiquette examples, a cybercrime case study, and a final project report. This project demonstrates the learning outcomes and practical knowledge gained throughout the course.

# Module Summaries
Module 1: Digital Literacy--Introduction to digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, and email etiquette.
Module 2: Digital Portfolio--Creation of online profiles and development of a digital portfolio to showcase skills and learning.
Module 3: Learning Platforms--Exploration of different online learning platforms and documentation of activities and learning outcomes.
Module 4: Email Etiquette--Understanding professional email writing, formatting, and communication guidelines.
Module 5: Cyber Safety & Cybercrime--Study of cybercrime cases, online safety practices, and prevention methods for secure internet usage.
