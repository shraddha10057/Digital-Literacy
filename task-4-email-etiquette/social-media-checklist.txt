       ***SOCIAL-MEDIA-DO'S & DON'TS-CHECKLIST***


✅ Do's

✔ Think before posting content online.
✔ Keep your privacy settings secure.
✔ Use strong passwords for your accounts.
✔ Share accurate and verified information.
✔ Be respectful in comments and messages.
✔ Follow professional behavior online.
✔ Report suspicious or harmful content.


❌ Don'ts

✘ Don't share personal information publicly.
✘ Don't post offensive or inappropriate content.
✘ Don't accept requests from unknown people.
✘ Don't click on suspicious links.
✘ Don't spread rumors or fake news.
✘ Don't share OTPs or passwords.
✘ Don't engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.
